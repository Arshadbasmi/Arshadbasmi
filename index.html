Apple Integration

This app hands work to Apple's apps, and iCloud syncs it to every device:

• Events → "Add to Apple Calendar" downloads an invite; open it and it lands in Apple Calendar on all your devices.
• Contacts → "Add to Apple Contacts" downloads a card; open it to save into Apple Contacts everywhere.
• Email → Send opens Apple Mail with your message ready, from your real accounts.
• Calls → the 📞 button dials through your iPhone/Mac.

Make sure iCloud sync is on for Calendar and Contacts on each device (System Settings → your name → iCloud).

Use on iPhone / iPad / Mac

Open this page in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen (or Dock on Mac) to install it like a native Apple app.
